# Toman-Bike-Dashboard
**Built an interactive Power BI dashboard for Toman Bike Shop using SQL and DAX. Analyzed $5M+ revenue, 1M+ riders, profit margins, seasonal trends, and customer demographics. Implemented KPIs, time-series visualizations, filters, slicers, and drill-downs to support data-driven business decisions.
Sales Dashboard for Toman Bike Shop**

Using Power BI and SQL, an interactive business intelligence dashboard was created for Toman Bike Shop to examine profitability, revenue trends, customer demographics, and sales performance.

**Important Features :**
SQL queries and Power BI were used to connect and transform sales data.
Constructed KPI cards to monitor:
Total Income ($5M+)
Total Number of Riders (1M+)
Margin of Profit
To examine monthly revenue, profit, and rider patterns, time-series graphics were made.
Year-based filtering was put into place for reporting and dynamic comparison.
To find times of high revenue, seasonal sales performance was examined.
Customer demographics were visualized, with a distinction made between casual and registered riders.
Drill-down features and interactive slicers were added for improved business insights.
Technologies Employed
Power BI: Dashboard creation and data visualization
SQL: Data extraction, cleansing, and transformation DAX: Business metrics and KPI computations

**Business Worth :**
In order to increase revenue and operational efficiency, the dashboard assists stakeholders in tracking sales performance, identifying profitable times, comprehending consumer behavior, and making data-driven decisions.
